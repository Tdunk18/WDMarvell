Downloading WD Mobile Apps

You can access content stored on the My Cloud device from a smartphone or tablet using the following  WD mobile apps:

    • My Albums: Lets you view and manage your photos.

    • My Cloud: Lets you view and play photos, videos, music, and files.

These free apps are available from Apple's App Store, Google Play, Windows Store, and Amazon.com.

Simply download these apps on each mobile device you want to use to access your device.
