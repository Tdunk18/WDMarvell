Backing Up Your Data with Apple Time Machine

You can back up your files on your My Cloud device to Apple Time Machine on a Mac computer.

Use the following steps to back up your data to Apple Time Machine.

    1. Click Settings.
    2. In the left pane, click General.
    3. In the MAC Backups area, click to turn on the Time Machine backup option.
    4. Click Configure.
    5. In the Select a Share dropdown list, select the share where your backed up files will reside.
    6. Click Save.
