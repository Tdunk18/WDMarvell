Welcome

Your My Cloud personal cloud storage system is a high-performance system providing secure wireless access to your media and files from any computer, tablet, or smartphone when connected to a wireless router. Your My Cloud device is the central component of your personal cloud and provides a simple, robust interface for easy setup, sharing, and maintenance.

User Roles in Your Personal Cloud

Everyone who accesses your personal cloud must have a user account for the My Cloud drive. As the My Cloud owner, you have a special user account (admin) that gives you administrator privileges. With these privileges, you can set up and configure the device to your specific needs, and add other users to your personal cloud. You have the power to determine exactly who can access the device.

NOTE: As a default, the admin user name does not have a password. You can add one anytime.

Important: You have the opportunity to change the administrator name and set an optional password while running the My Cloud setup software or completing the Getting Started screens. Accessing Content from a Smartphone or Tablet

Two WD mobile apps allow you to access the contents of your My Cloud device from your smartphone or tablet:

    • My Albums: View and manage your photos.

    • My Cloud: View and play photos, videos, music, and files.

Get these free apps from the WD Learning Center (http://www.wd.com/setup/) or other app sites such as  Apple App Store, Google Play, Windows Store, and Amazon.com.

Mobile Device Access Workflow for You (the Administrator)

The basic workflow for you as the My Cloud owner/administrator:

1

Configure the My Cloud device for remote access and sign up for a MyCloud.com login. How to

2

Download the WD mobile apps. How to

3

Perform one-time setup for secure access. How to

4

Launch a WD app and access the device.

5

Viola! Play and view your content.

Mobile Device Access Workflow for Others

NOTE:When you add a user to your My Cloud, you also provide their email address to MyCloud.com, creating a My Cloud account. Once created, an email is sent to the new user's email address containing a  link to My Cloud allowing them to continue with the registration process.

Accessing Your Content from a Laptop

Use the My Cloud desktop application to access the content of the My Cloud device on your Mac or PC. My Cloud lets you remotely access the photos, videos, music, and files stored on your WD NAS devices, including My Cloud and My Book Live drives, that are connected to your home network. As with your WD mobile app, you need the all-important MyCloud.com login to access your content.

Download the My Cloud desktop application at http://www.wd.com/setup/.

For step-by-step instructions, see Accessing Content from Your Laptop.

Displaying the My Cloud Interface

The My Cloud device user interface lets you set up and manage your personal cloud. The interface displays in a web browser running on your Mac or PC. 

To display the web interface:

    1. Open a browser window.
    2. In the Address bar, enter http:// <MyCloud device name>;
      Example: http://MyCloud
      NOTE: If the URL fails to display the web interface, trying entering this URL: http://MyCloud.local
