Creating Volume Virtualization

Volume Virtualization simplifies scalability by allowing you to map iSCSI targets on other network storage devices and present them as volumes on your My Cloud device.

Use the following steps to create Volume Virtualization on your device.

    1. Click Storage.
    2. Click Volume Virtualization.
    3. In the right pane, click Create.
    4. In the Device IP field, enter the IP address of the device where the iSCSI target resides, then click Next.
    5. Select an iSCSI target from the retrieved list and click Next.
    6. To add authentication to the target, click the toggle button, enter a User Name and Password and  click Next.
    7. Disabled authentication is the default. To keep the default, ensure that the Authentication field is Off, then click Next.
    8. Select a LUN (Local Unit Number) from the list, then click Next.
    9. Enter a name for the share folder, then click Next..
    10. Review the Volume Virtualization summary to ensure that your settings are correct and click Save. A virtual volume is created.
