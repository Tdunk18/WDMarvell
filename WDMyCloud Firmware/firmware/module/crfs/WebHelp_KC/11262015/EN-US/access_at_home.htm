Accessing Content at Home

When you are at home and your computing devices are connected to your home network, you access the content stored on your drive slightly differently than the remote access ways. When you're connected to your home network, you can:

    • Access your content from a smartphone or tablet
    • Access your content from a computer

Important: Before you begin, make sure you completed the steps described in Setting Up Cloud Access.

Accessing Content from a Mobile Device Connected to Your Home Network

    1. If you haven't already done so, download the WD mobile apps.
    2. On your smartphone or tablet, connect to your home network.
      If you don't know how to do this, refer to the documentation for your device.
    3. Launch a WD mobile app (My Cloud or My Albums).
    4. If prompted, tap Connect Now.
    5. Select your My Cloud drive.
    6. Select the share you want to access and then navigate to your desired content.

Accessing Content from a Computer Connected to Your Home Network

    1. Make sure your computer is connected to your home network and that you have Internet access.
    2. If you haven't already done so, download the My Cloud desktop application from the WD Learning Center (http://www.wd.com/setup/) and install it on your Mac or PC.
    3. On your computer, launch the My Cloud desktop application.
    4. Enter your login credentials.
    5. Double-click the My Cloud drive.
    6. In the left pane, expand folders as necessary to navigate to the desired content.
