Creating a USB Backup

Use the following steps to backup your data on your My Cloud to or from a USB device.

    1. Click Backups.
    2. Click USB Backups.
    3. Click Create Job.
    4. Enter a Job Name for your backup.
    5. Indicate the direction of your backup from the pull-down menu. Options include:
    • USB to NAS: Backs up the data on your USB device to the My Cloud device.
    • NAS to USB: Backs up the data on your My Cloud device to a USB device.
    1. Select the Source Folder for your backup:
    • Click Browse and select the folder you'd like to backup from your destination device.
    1. Select the Destination Folder for your backup.
    • Click Browse and select the folder to which you'd like to back up your source device.
    1. Click Create. This creates the backup job for the requested backup
    2. In the USB Backup Jobs, click the arrow to begin your backup.
    3. Click Details in the USB Backup Jobs area to check the progress of your backup job.
