Remote Access Basics

There are three ways you can access the content stored on My Cloud from anywhere::

    • From a mobile device such as a smartphone or tablet.  
    • From your computer (most likely your laptop).  

Mobile Device Access Overview

Use the following steps to access your cloud from a smartphone or tablet:

1

Get a MyCloud.com login. This is normally done at the time you add a user to your device. See Setting Up Cloud Access.

2

Download and install the free My Albums and My Cloud apps onto you mobile device.
These apps are available from the Apple App Store and Google Play.

3

Perform one-time setup for secure access. See Performing One-Time Setup for Secure Access

4

Access your content from the mobile device. See Accessing Content from a Smartphone or Tablet

Windows or Mac Desktop Access Overview

When you are away from home (such as in a coffee shop), you can easily access your device from your laptop. The My Cloud desktop application, a  Wi-Fi enabled laptop, and a Wi-Fi connection are required for remote access.

Use the following steps to access your cloud with My Cloud desktop application:

1

Get a MyCloud.com login. This is normally done at the time you add a user to your device. See Setting Up Cloud Access.

2

Download the My Cloud desktop application from the WD Learning Center (http://www.wd.com/setup/) and install it on your computer.

3

Perform one-time setup for secure access. See Performing One-Time Setup for Secure Access

4

Access your content from your laptop. See Accessing Content from your Laptop
