Accessing Content from a Smartphone or Tablet

You can use your smartphone or tablet to access the content stored on your My Cloud device. Before you can do this, you must have already completed the following tasks: Setting Up Cloud Access, Downloading WD Mobile Apps, and Performing One-Time Setup for Secure Access.

Use the following steps to access content from a smartphone or tablet.

    1. Ensure that your mobile device has a Wi-Fi or 3G/4G connection.
    2. Launch a WD mobile app (My Cloud or My Albums).
    3. If prompted, tap Connect Now.
    4. Select your My Cloud drive.
    5. Select the share you want to access and then navigate to your desired content.
