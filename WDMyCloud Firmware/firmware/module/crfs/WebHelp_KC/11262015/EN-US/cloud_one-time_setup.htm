Performing One-Time Setup for Secure Access

This procedure explains how to establish a secure connection between your smartphone or tablet and your My Cloud device. Use the instructions for your specific mobile device:

    • Android Smartphone or Tablet  

    • iPhone or iPad

    • Windows Phone or Tablet

    • Windows or Mac Desktop

Getting Secure Access for an Android Smartphone or Tablet

    1. Connect your smartphone or tablet by doing the following:

      1. On your mobile device, tap Settings.

      2. Tap Wireless & Networks.

      3. Tap Wi-Fi Settings.

      4. Select your home network.

      5. Enter your network password and tap Connect.

      6. Launch a WD app.
      7. Tap Connect Now.
      8. Tap the name of the My Cloud device.
      9. Tap the drop-down arrow and select your user name.
      10. Do one of the following:

    • If a password was set for that user, enter it and then tap Login.

    • If a password was not set for the user, tap Login.

    1. (Optional) If you want to access any of your content, tap a share name and then navigate to the desired file.

Getting Secure Access for an iPhone or iPad

    1. Connect your smartphone or tablet by doing the following:

    1. On your mobile device, tap Settings.

    1. Tap Wi-Fi.

    2. Tap Wi-Fi Settings.

    3. Select your home network.

    4. Enter your network password and tap Join.

    5. Launch a WD app.
    6. Tap Connect Now.
    7. Tap the plus sign (+) next to the name of the My Cloud device.
    8. Tap the desired user name.
      If a password was set for  the user, enter it.
    9. (Optional) If you want to access any of your content, tap a share name and then navigate to the desired file.

Getting Secure Access for a Windows Smartphone or Tablet

    1. Connect your smartphone or tablet by doing the following:

    1. On your computing device, tap Settings.

    1. Tap Wi-Fi.

    2. Select your home network.

    3. Enter your network password and tap Done.

    1. Launch a WD app.
    2. Tap Connect Now.
    3. Tap the name of the My Cloud drive.
    4. Tap the drop-down arrow and select a user name.
      If a password was set for the user, enter it.
    5. (Optional) If you want to access any of your content, do the following:

    1. On the Devices screen, tap the name of your My Cloud drive.

    2. Tap a share and then navigate to your desired content.

Getting Secure Access for a Windows or Mac Desktop or Laptop

    1. After you install the My Cloud for desktop app , double-click the shortcut that now appears on your desktop to display all of the WD devices on the network. For information on installing the app, see Remote Access Basics.
    2. The system may display information about security alerts. To avoid displaying this screen again, select the I understand checkbox.
    3. Click I understand to display devices on your network.
    4. Click the device you want to connect to, in this case the My Cloud device. A Sign In screen displays.
    5. Enter the password to the My Cloud device, if you set one up earlier, and click Sign In. The Getting Started screen displays.
    6. To obtain more information, click the WD Learning Center link.
    7. Click Start to display the My Cloud device’s  shares and any SmartWare or Time Machine backup files.
