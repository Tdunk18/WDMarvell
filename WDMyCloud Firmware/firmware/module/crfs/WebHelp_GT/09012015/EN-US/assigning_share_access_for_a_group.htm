Assigning Share Access for a Group

Once you’ve created a group, you can determine the level of access for the members of that group. Use the following steps to assign share access to a group.

    1. Click Users.
    2. Click Groups.
    3. In the left pane, select the Group to which you’d like to assign access. Group information displays in the Group Profile area.
    4. In the Share Access area, select the access level for each share on your device.
    • Read/write access. Select this option to allow full access to the selected information.
    • Read-only access. Allows the group member to read but not change the selected information.
    • Deny access. The group member has no access to this information.

Shares are now assigned to the selected group.
