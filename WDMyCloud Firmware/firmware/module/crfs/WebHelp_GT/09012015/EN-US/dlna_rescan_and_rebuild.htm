Rescanning/Rebuilding the DLNA Database

If you encounter a mismatch in the amount of My Cloud Mirror content accessible or viewable by a DLNA device, you can do either to the DLNA server database:

    • Rescan: My Cloud Mirror device searches for changes made to your media libraries. Try this first.
    • Rebuild: My Cloud Mirror device rebuilds the database from scratch.
      NOTE: Use caution when completing this procedure. Rebuilding the DLNA server database could take a considerable amount of time, depending on the number and size of the files involved.

Use the following steps to rescan or rebuild the DLNA database:

    1. Click Settings.
    2. In the left pane, click Media.
    3. In the DLNA Database field, click either Rescan or Rebuild.
