Auto-Rebuilding your RAID

The Auto-Rebuilding option allow you to automatically rebuild the RAID for your My Cloud Mirror device in case of drive corruption. Use the following steps to enable the auto-rebuild option.

    1. Click Storage.
    2. Click RAID.
    3. Review the Auto-Rebuild field to determine whether your My Cloud Mirror device is currently setup for auto-rebuild.
    4. Auto-Rebuild is currently enabled.
    5. Auto-Rebuild is currently disabled.
    1. Select to enable the Auto-Rebuild option if it is currently disabled.
