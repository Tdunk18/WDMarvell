Editing a Group Profile

Edit group quotas and group membership using the following steps:

    1. Click Users.
    2. Click Groups.
    3. Select the Group you’d like to edit.
    4. Click Configure next to the information you’d like to update.
    5. Edit the information you’d like to update.
    6. Click Apply. Your new group is now updated.
