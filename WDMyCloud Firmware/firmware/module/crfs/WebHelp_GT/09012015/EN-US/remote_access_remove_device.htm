Removing a Device

Use the following steps to remove a device from your cloud.

    1. Click Cloud Access.
    2. In the left pane, click the user whose device you want to delete.
    3. In the Cloud Devices section, locate the device.
      Status information reflects a connection (Connected) for WD Photos and/or My Cloud.
    4. Do one or more of the following:
    • For the corresponding WD Photos row, click the Delete icon to remove a device's access to the cloud through the WD Photos mobile application.
    • For the corresponding My Cloud row, click the Delete icon   to remove a device's access to the cloud through the My Cloud application.
    • For the row that corresponds to the mobile device, click the Delete icon   to remove the device from your My Cloud Mirror environment.
    1. In the confirmation dialog box, click Yes.
