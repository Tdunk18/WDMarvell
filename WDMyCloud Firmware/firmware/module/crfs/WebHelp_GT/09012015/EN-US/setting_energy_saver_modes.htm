Setting Energy Saver Modes

Reduce the energy consumption of your My Cloud Mirror device using the following steps.

    1. Click Settings.
    2. Click General.
    3. In the Energy Saver area, select the following options to set the energy consumption of your device.

Mode

Description

Drive Sleep

Enables/ disables the Sleep Mode on your device. When On is selected, the drive sleep mode is enabled.

  • Click    to enable/disable Drive Sleep.

LED

Enables/disables the LEDs on your device. When On is selected, LEDs are disabled.

  • Click    to enable/disable LED.

Power Recovery

Automatically restarts your device after an unexpected shutdown due to a power failure. When On is selected, Power Recovery is enabled.

  • Click    to enable/disable Power Recovery.

Power Schedule

Allows you to set a schedule indicating when the device will be shut down.

  • Click    to enable/disable Power Schedule.
  • Click Configure to set the schedule for your device.

Web Access Timeout

Set the default time that the device automatically logs you out of the device.

  • Select the Web Access Timeout time from the drop-down menu.
