Enabling/Disabling Apps

Before you can use an app, you have to enable it for use on your device. Once enabled, the app will display on the Apps page. Conversely if you want to remove the app from the Apps page but not delete it, you can disable it, removing it from the Apps page.

Use the following steps to enable/disable apps on your My Cloud Mirror device.

    1. Click Settings.
    2. Click Apps.
    3. In the Installed Apps area, select the app you’d like to enable/disable.
    4. Click Details.
    5. Click Enable or Disable.
    6. Click Close. Your app is now enabled/disabled.
