Uploading Content to the My Cloud Mirror Device

To upload photos, videos, music, and files to your My Cloud Mirror device, you use one of the following procedures:

    • Mac
    • Windows PC
    • USB

Uploading Content from a Mac

Use the following steps to upload files from your Mac onto your My Cloud Mirror device:

    1. On your Mac, choose Go > Network from the menu bar.
    2. Do one of the following:
    • In the left pane of the Finder, double-click the name of the My Cloud Mirror device.
    • In the right pane, locate the name of the  My Cloud Mirror device and double-click it.
    1. Double-click the name of a particular share to open the folder and view any existing subfolders. (Public is a share created automatically.)
    2. Drag and drop files from your computer to the desired folder.
      Do not drop files into the Time Machine and WD SmartWare Backups folders.
      NOTE: If you copy files from a computer to the My Cloud Mirror device, those files will reside in two places: in the original location and on your device.

Uploading Content from a PC

Use the following steps to upload files from your Windows PC to the My Cloud Mirror device:

    1. On your computer, open a Run dialog box by pressing the Windows and R keys at the same time.
    2. In the Open field, enter \\ followed by the name of the My Cloud Mirror device. (The default name is mycloudmirror, without the spaces.) Example: \\mycloudmirror
    3. Click OK.
      Windows Explorer or File Explorer opens with available shares listed in the right pane.
    4. Double-click the name of a particular share to open the folder and view any subfolders. (Public is a share created automatically.)
    5. Drag and drop files from your computer to this share.
      NOTE: If you copy files from computer to the My Cloud Mirror device, those files will reside in two places: in the original location and on your device.

     

    Uploading Content from a USB

    Use the following steps to upload files from a USB to the My Cloud Mirror device:

    1. Connect the USB drive to your computer for optimal performance.

    2. Connect your computer and the My Cloud Mirror device directly to your router with a wired Ethernet connection.

    3. Copy the data from your external USB drive to your My Cloud Mirror device

NOTE: If you copy files from computer to the My Cloud Mirror device, those files will reside in two places: in the original location and on your device.
