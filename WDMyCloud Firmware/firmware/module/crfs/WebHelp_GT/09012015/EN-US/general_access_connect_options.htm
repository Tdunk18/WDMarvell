Configuring Connection Options

You can specify a connection option that determines how mobile devices and computers will access the My Cloud Mirror device remotely.

Use the following steps to configure the connection options.

    1. Click Settings.
    2. In the left pane, click General.
    3. In the Cloud Access area, Cloud Service field, click Configure.
    4. In the dialog box, click one of the three settings:
    • Auto: Establishes a direct connection between your mobile device and router. If the connection cannot open ports through uPnP (Universal Plug and Play), a relay connection is established.
    • Manual: Establishes a connection through two ports you choose. This option sets up a direct connection through port forwarding. For step-by-step instructions on using this option, see Enabling Port Forwarding. As part of using this option, you must manually configure your router accordingly.
    • Win XP: Establishes a connection through ports 80 and 443. If these ports are unavailable, a relay connection is established. You must choose this option if at least one of your remote devices runs the Windows XP operating system.
    1. Click Apply.
