Connecting the My Cloud Mirror Device to Your Network

This procedure explains how to connect the My Cloud Mirror drive to your home network using an Ethernet cable. After making this initial connection, you can configure the drive to connect to your network wirelessly.

Use the following steps to connect with an Ethernet cable.

    1. Connect one end of the cable into the Ethernet port on the back of the My Cloud Mirror.device.
    1. Connect the other end of the cable directly into a router or network switch port.
    2. Connect the end of the adapter into the power supply socket on the back of the drive.
    3. Plug the power adapter into a power outlet. The My Cloud Mirror device powers up automatically.

Important: Wait for the  My Cloud Mirror device  to finish powering up (approximately three minutes) before configuring the connector.

After you connect the  My Cloud Mirror device to your network via an Ethernet cable, you can use the web interface to set up a wireless connection. See Configuring Connection Options.
