Deleting an FTP Download

Use the following steps to delete a scheduled FTP download.

    1. Click Apps.
    2. Click FTP Downloads.
    3. In the FTP Downloads Queue, select the download you’d like to delete.
    4. Click Delete.
    5. Review the warning screen, then click OK. Your download is deleted and removed from the FTP Downloads Queue.
