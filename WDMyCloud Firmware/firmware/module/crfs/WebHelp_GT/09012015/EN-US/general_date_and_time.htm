Setting the Date and Time

Use the following steps to set the date and time for your My Cloud Mirror device.

Setting the Time Zone

    1. Click Settings.
    2. In the left pane, click General.
    3. In the Time Zone field, Select a time zone from the dropdown menu.
    4. Click Save.

Synchronizing the Date and Time to NTP

    1. Click Settings
    2. In the left pane, click General.
    3. Click to turn the NTP Service On.
    4. Determine the servers to be used by the NTP Service. How do I do this?    

       

      To configure NTP service:

      1. Click the Configure link next to the Primary Server field.
      2. In the NTP Settings dialog box, click Add User NTP.
      3. Enter the server details, and then click Save.
      4. Repeat steps 1 and 2 as necessary. You can enter a maximum of three NTP servers.
