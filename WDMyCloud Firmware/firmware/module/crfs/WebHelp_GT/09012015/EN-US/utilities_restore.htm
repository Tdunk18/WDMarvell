Restoring Default Settings

The system restore allows the Administrator to perform a full factory restore on the My Cloud Mirror device. This option restores your device to factory default settings with no data loss.

Before performing a restore, you can save and export your current configuration (How to) and then import it later (How to).

Important: A restore disrupts service to users currently accessing your cloud. Do not unplug the My Cloud Mirror device while a restore is in progress.

Use the following steps to restore the  My Cloud Mirror device.

    1. Click Settings.
    2. In the left pane, click Utilities.
    3. In the System Restore area, click System Restore.
    4. Review the configuration message, then click OK. The device will reboot. Don’t unplug the device during this rebooting process. Once the reboot is complete, launch the Dashboard.
